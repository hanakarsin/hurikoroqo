Kiraithe sacked as former Operation Linda Nchi spokesman takes over as government spokesman

Government spokesperson Eric Kiraithe has been sacked and his position taken up by former Operation Linda Nchi spokesman Cyrus Oguna.

In a statement signed by ICT Cabinet Secretary Joe Mucheru, the move takes effect immediately as the government continues to streamline its communication structure.

According to the press release by the ministry dated Tuesday, May 7, the new appointee will be charged with the responsibility of overseeing all government communication channels under the ministry of ICT.

Some of these channels include; Directorate of Information and all county and sub county information officers, Department of Public Information, National Government Communication Centre and the Government Media centre.

"I congratulate Colonel (RTD) Cyprus Odhiambo Oguna on his appointment and pledge the Government's support, as he takes up this challenging but exciting role," said the CS in a statement

Oguna will also have a content oversight role over the two government news gathering and dissemination channels, Kenya News Agency (KNA) and MyGov.

He came into public limelight when Kenya Defence Forces crossed border into Somalia to protect the country against al shabaab militia and help restore peace in the horn of Africa.

As a spokesperson of the troops, Oguna enthusiastically communicated the progress made by the forces from time to time and handled challenging questions from journalists.
