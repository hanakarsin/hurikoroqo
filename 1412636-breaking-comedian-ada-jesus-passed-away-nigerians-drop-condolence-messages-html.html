Breaking: Comedian Ada Jesus has Passed Away, Nigerians Drop Condolence Messages

- Nigerians have taken to social media to mourn the death of comedian Ada Jesus

- The comedian reportedly died on the morning of Wednesday, April 21

- Ada Jesus battled with kidney disease for many months before her death

Condolence messages are pouring in for the family of Ada Jesus as the comedian passed away on the morning of Wednesday, April 21.

Human Rights activist Gwamnishu Emefiena Harrison, who has been taking care of the comedian, confirmed to Legit.ng via a phone call that Ada is dead.

A lady called Maria Ude Nwachi also shared a photo of the comedian with the message of her death on Facebook.
