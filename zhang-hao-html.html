Zhang Hao (Figure Skater) - Age, Family, Bio

Chinese pair skater who has gained fame for his career skating with Yu Xiaoyu, including as the winner at the 2018 Chinese Figure Skating Championships. He also won the 2017 Asian Winter Games with her. With his partner, he became a 2006 Olympic silver medalist.

Before Fame

He began skating competitively at the junior level in 1998. He first paired with Zhang Dan, with whom he won a gold medal at the 1998-99 Junior Grand Prix. 

Trivia

He finished as a silver medalist skating in a pair with Yu Xiaoyu at the 2017 Grand Prix Final. The pair also finished second at the Four Continents Championships in 2015. 

Family Life

He shares a son with his wife Ju Chi. 

Associated With

He and Eric Radford both competed in pair figure skating at the 2018 Winter Olympics.
