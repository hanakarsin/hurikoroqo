Bindi Irwin - Age, Family, Bio

Daughter of the famous Crocodile Hunter, Steve Irwin. She hosted the show Jungle Girl from 2007 to 2008 and also hosted a show called Bindi's Bootcamp. In 2015, she won the popular ABC series Dancing with the Stars. She and her family were given their own reality series on Animal Planet called Crikey! It's the Irwins in 2018.

Before Fame

She appeared on the television show The Wiggles.

Trivia

Her first name comes from a famous crocodile and her middle name, Sue, is the same name as their family dog.

Family Life

Her parents are Steve and Terri Irwin. She has a younger brother named Robert. She began dating Chandler Powell in 2015 and the two got married on March 25, 2020. In March 2021 the couple welcomed a baby daughter named Grace Warrior Irwin Powell.

Associated With

She was in the movie Free Willy: Escape from Pirate's Cove alongside Beau Bridges.
