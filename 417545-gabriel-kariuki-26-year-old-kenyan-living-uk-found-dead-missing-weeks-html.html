The search for a missing UK-based Kenyan national has tragically ended after his decomposing body was discovered.

Cleveland police officers on Friday, June 25, found the body at the Seaton Carew beach after which investigations established it was Gabriel Kariuki who had been reported missing.

According to UK publication, Hartlepool Mail, Kariki was last seen on Monday, May 18, a missing person report was filed on the following day prompting the authorities to launch the search.

At the time of his disappearance, the 26-year-old was living in Hartlepool where he moved in recently to live with his mother.

The deceased graduated from the University of Birmingham in 2017 with hopes of becoming a quantity surveyor.

Following the tragic news, family and friends started an online fundraiser for his funeral and other expenses.

"Gabriel touched our hearts in a way we will never be able to describe......Thank you to all who helped us search for Gabriel. We are hoping to put together a befitting send-off for our beautiful boy. We would appreciate your help, your thoughts and your prayers," the fundraising committee appeal.

The 26-year-old basketball player Keegan Oyugi who went missing in Minnesota was found dead in the wreckage of a car and according to the police, the description of the body and clothing matched his.

According to the statement from the Minnetrista Police Department, Oyugi was the only occupant in the car.

"The crash remains under investigation. As we await formal identification from the medical examiner’s office, our thoughts and prayers go out to the Oyugi family,” the police department said.
