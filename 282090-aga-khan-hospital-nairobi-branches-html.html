Aga Khan hospital Nairobi branches

For more than five decades, Aga Khan University Hospital (AKUH) has been taking care of a good number of families in East Africa. The hospital's vision is to be the tertiary, premier, teaching and referral health care hospital in sub-Saharan Africa. As a non-profit private hospital, it strives to offer quality health care to everyone. Aga Khan Hospital Nairobi branches are located in different accessible areas.

The hospital's first priority is to serve the patients effectively. Their highly proficient team is always ready to provide you and your loved ones with the best healthcare standards. Branches of Aga Khan Hospital in Nairobi are dedicated to working together to make sure you receive first-rate facilities, outstanding medical services as well as compassionate care.

Aga Khan University hospital Nairobi branches offer topnotch standard to all their clients.
