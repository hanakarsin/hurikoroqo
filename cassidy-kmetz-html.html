Cassidy Kmetz - Age, Family, Bio

Influencer and fitness coach who became known for her cassidykmetz TikTok account. She posts recipes, workouts crafted for specific body parts, motivation, and best practices for exercise. Her page has garnered over 260,000 followers.

Before Fame

She joined TikTok in September of 2019. 

Trivia

Her cassidykmetz Instagram account features selfies and product endorsements. One of her most popular videos is about personalized protein powder. It has earned over 25 million views. Her self-titled YouTube channel is a place where features her workout routines, as well as shares her journey into fitness.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Pennsylvania. She posted her significant other Joey Haggerty on her Instagram page in 2022.

Associated With

On October 31, 2019, she posted a TikTok video set to "Take Your Man" by Mahogany LOX.
