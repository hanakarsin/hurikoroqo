African beauty: Nameless showers his twa-mate Wahu with praise as she turns year older

- Nameless has sent a lovely message to his wife and singer Wahu on her birthday

- The father of two wished Wahu would continue to understand her gifts and passions and use them to inspire and impact lives

- Wahu also shared a message on her Instagram thanking God for life, health and family

Singer Nameless has penned down a beautiful message to wife Wahu Kagwi on her birthday.

The Sinzia hitmaker shared his message on Instagram attaching several photos of him and his wife together also attaching pictures of their beautiful children.

Nameless wished Wahu would continue to understand her gifts and passions and use them to inspire and impact lives.

"Mama-Yao, my life-partner, team-mate, Twa-mate. May you continue discovering your inner brilliance and wisdom.
