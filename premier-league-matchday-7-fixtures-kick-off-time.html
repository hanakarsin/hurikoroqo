Premier League Matchday 7 Fixtures, Kick-Off Time

The 2023-2024 Premier League matchday 7 will kick off with Aston Villa vs Brighton clash at Villa Park on Saturday, September 30.

Aston Villa are going into the game with mixed results as they beat Chelsea 1-0 in a Premier League game at Stamford Bridge last weekend but suffered a 1-2 defeat at the hands of Everton on Wednesday in the Carabao Cup third round.

Brighton had a similar experience as they enjoyed a 3-1 Premier League victory over Bournemouth last weekend but suffered a 1-0 defeat at the hands of Chelsea in the Carabao Cup third round.

After the Aston Villa vs. Brighton clash, there will be six Premier League games lined up for 3 p.m. kick-off time on Saturday. The games are Manchester United vs. Crystal Palace, Newcastle United vs. Burnley, Wolves vs. Manchester City, Bournemouth vs. Arsenal, West Ham vs Sheffield United, and Everton vs Luton Town.
