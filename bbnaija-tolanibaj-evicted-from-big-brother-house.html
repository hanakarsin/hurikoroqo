Tolanibaj Evicted From Big Brother House

Tolanibaj has been evicted from the ongoing Big Brother House.

Naija News reports that while viewers including the housemates expected only bottom four housemates to be up for eviction, the show host, Ebuka told all the housemates except the Head of House Ozo and Deputy HOH Nengi to stand up during the live eviction show on Sunday.

After this, the housemate with the least number of votes, Tolanibaj was evicted immediately without the opportunity of the housemates voting for who to keep or evict from the house.

Meanwhile, Big Brother Naija season five is tagged “BBNaija Lockdown“. Since the beginning of this season, seven housemates have been evicted so far namely, Lilo, Ka3na, Eric, Tochi, Kaisha, Praise and Tolanibaj.

Lilo and Ka3na were the first two housemates to be evicted, followed by Eric and Tochi. Kaisha became the fifth housemate to be evicted from BBNaija House followed by Praise and Tolanibaj this week who became the seventh housemate to be evicted from the BBNaija Lockdown House.
