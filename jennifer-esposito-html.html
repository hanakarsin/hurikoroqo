Jennifer Esposito - Age, Family, Bio

American actress best known for her roles as Andrea on Samantha Who? and as Jackie on Blue Bloods. She received additional fame for her appearance in the feature films Crash, Don't Say a Word, and Taxi. 

Before Fame

She won the title of homecoming queen in high school.

Trivia

She was diagnosed with Celiac Disease and has developed her own line of gluten-free foods.

Family Life

She was married to Bradley Cooper from 2006 until 2007. She married Louis Dowler in 2014. 

Associated With

She played a supporting role in the 1998 slasher film I Still Know What You Did Last Summer, which starred Jennifer Love Hewitt.
