Loann Kaji - Age, Family, Bio

Mother of Ryan Kaji, who has been featured on the popular YouTube channels Ryan's World and Ryan's Family Review. She also appeared in the Nickelodeon series Ryan's Mystery Playdate. She has worked as the managing partner for her family owned Sunlight Entertainment LLC. 

Before Fame

She and her husband raised Ryan in Cypress, Texas. She was a student at Texas Tech University where she met her husband.

Trivia

In 2002, she was arrested for theft of clothes at a JC Penney. She became a high school chemistry teacher.

Family Life

She and her husband Shion Kaji married in August of 2013. They have twin daughters named Emma and Kate along with their son Ryan. 

Associated With

Her son booked his own show with pocket.watch like Marlin Ramsey Chan. His was called Ryan Toys Review Ultimate mishmash while hers was titled MarMar Land.
