Nadia Mukami: 10 cutest photos of the Kenyan singer-songwriter

Nadia Mukami is one of the most talented female singers in Kenya. She is not only vocally gifted, but her songwriting skills are also at another level. Her love songs have made it to the favourite playlists of East African fans and those from countries that do not speak Swahili, such as Nigeria. It is impossible not to love this musical goddess who also happens to be the epitome of beauty.

Nadia Mukami's life represents a lady who strived to achieve the prowess needed to be a commanding voice in the music industry. The award-winning musician brought a spectacular touch of music to the Kenyan music industry. Her natural talent combined with afro-pop rendition is just what you need to have on your playlist.
