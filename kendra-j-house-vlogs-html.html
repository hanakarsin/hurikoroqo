Kendra Johnston - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube vlogger who runs the J House Vlogs channel with her husband Jeremy. They homeschool their children and post vlog entries providing a glimpse into their lives. They have received well over 100 million collective views on their tornado related videos. 

Before Fame

She created the J House Vlogs YouTube channel on August 14, 2014. She posted the first vlog entry there on September 18, 2014.  

Trivia

Her channel has garnered more than 2.9 million subscribers since its founding. The family Instagram page has received over 160,000 followers for its wholesome family photographs. 

Family Life

She has five children including Isaac, Elise, Caleb, Laura and Janae, who are often featured in the channel's videos. They are based in Kansas City, Missouri.

Associated With

She and fellow YouTuber Shay Butler are both known for their family-centric vlogging channels.
