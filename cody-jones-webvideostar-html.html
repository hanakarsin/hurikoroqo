Cody Jones (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

One of the five co-founders of the enormously popular YouTube channel Dude Perfect, which has earned over 57 million subscribers. The channel is a sports and entertainment brand that specializes in trick shots and comedy. 

Before Fame

He came together with Coby and Cory Cotton, Tyler Toney, and Garrett Hilbert to form the YouTube channel in June of 2009 while attending Texas A&M University. 

Trivia

He is the Tall Guy of Dude Perfect and is often the loudest member as well. He loves playing golf and was featured in the "All Sports Golf Battle 2 | Dude Perfect" video from December of 2017.

Family Life

He was born in Plano, Texas. He got married to his wife Allison. In June 2016, he and Allison welcomed a daughter named Landry. They later welcomed a daughter named Lucy in July 2018. Their third child, a son named Easton, arrived in December 2020.

Associated With

He and Garrett Hilbert are both members of Dude Perfect.
