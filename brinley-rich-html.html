Brinley Rich - Age, Family, Bio

Member of the YouTube vlogging family The Riches, she appears alongside her parents and siblings on their popular channel It's R Life. The family has gained more than 1.3 million subscribers documenting their everyday life. She was featured in the "Evie Brinley and Kapri's 1st Cheer Performance! New Team!" video from November of 2021. 

Before Fame

Her family channel originated as The Rich family in August 2016 before rebranding to It's R Life two years later.

Trivia

Outside their YouTube channel, the Rich family can be found posting family and promotional photos on their therich.life Instagram. She has enjoyed Cheerleading and has posted multiple photos about it on her personal Instagram. 

Family Life

She appears on The Rich Life with her parents, Kendal and Daniel, and her siblings Evie, Sebastian, Kapri, and Hampton. 

Associated With

Like Isabelle Ingham, she gained fame appearing on a popular family YouTube channel.
