Bomet: Form Four Student, 24, Killed in Bar after Brawl over Woman

  • The Form Four Student from Bomet was involved in a deadly fight with an unknown person in a bar
  • The two were fighting over a woman when the unknown man unleashed a sharp knife and stabbed the 24-year-old high school student
  • The woman they were fighting over fled the scene after the tragic incident

A 24-year-old Form Four student from Kapsinendet Secondary School in Bomet has been stabbed to death after a fight over a woman in a bar turned deadly.

The young man was involved in an altercation with an unknown person over a woman and the fight quickly degenerated into violence as the other man unleashed a sharp knife.

According to a report by Citizen TV, the unknown man shoved his weapon into the student's neck, twice, leaving him with deep cuts; well-wishers tried rushing him to a medical facility but he succumbed to the injuries.

Similarly, detectives also arrested a murder suspect who stabbed his friend in the neck, killing him on the spot, after a short argument.

The suspect, Daniel Waithera, aged 25, is said to have killed his friend Michael Mukami, 21, after a tiff over who was to pay for fare after a motorbike ride.

Taking to Twitter on Sunday, September 27, DCI said the motorbike rider, Stephen Mwango, said he had picked the two at around 5.30am, at Gikambura market in Kikuyu, Kiambu county.
