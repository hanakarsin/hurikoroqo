Michael and Chloe's Decision Day on 'Married at First Sight' Ends Their Journey Together

Chloe was technically Michael's second bride in Season 17 of 'Married at First Sight.'

By Kelly Corbett

Apr. 3 2024, Updated 7:54 a.m. ET

Season 17 of Married At First Sight is easily one of the most chaotic seasons of the series, and Decision Day really brings the drama. Becca Haley and Austin Reed, Emily Balch and Brennan Shoykhet, and Clare Kerr and Cameron Frazer all meet with the experts to make their final decision about their relationships.

Meanwhile, we learn that Lauren Goodger and Orion Martzloff had already called it quits by that point. But there's one couple we don't get to hear from on the standard Decision Day date: Chloe Brown and Michael Shiakallis. However, later, they do get to have their own Decision Day, and we have all the info about that.

What happens on Michael and Chloe's Decision Day on 'Married at First Sight'?

Since MAFS gave Michael another shot at love after his initial match left him at the altar, he and Chloe aren't been together as long as the other couples this season. That said, they have more time to continue putting their relationship to the test after the initial Season 17 Decision Day for the other pairs.

Given their late start, it was important to Pastor Cal and the experts that they didn't rush Michael and Chloe. And toward the end of the season, we finally see Chloe and Michael's Decision Day.

Despite her concerns about their marriage, Chloe actually says yes to staying together. However, Michael says in the episode, he isn't as ready as he thought he was to be in a marriage. And since both parties have to agree to remain married, these two are among the other divorced couples of the season.

Are Michael and Chloe still together?

It's unclear if Michael and Chloe are still together on MAFS. They have very different styles, and Michael's is somewhat unconventional and not a turn on for her. "I'm not used to a man wearing my earrings," she admits in an episode after Michael says he wants to wear one of her earrings in his ear.
