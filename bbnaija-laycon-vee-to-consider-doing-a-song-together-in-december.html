BBNaija Laycon, Vee To Consider Doing A Song Together In December

Nigerian blogger, Noble Igwe has encouraged former housemates in the recently concluded season of the Big Brother Naija reality show, Laycon and Vee to do a song together before the year runs out.

Igwe in a post sited by Naija News on his verified microblogging site account wished that the duo could get into the studio in December and release a song that will bang.

He wrote; “I really wish @veeiye & @itsLaycon will get into a studio as e dey hot.
Thing is, if they can, they may put out something that will bang this Detty December.”

See the post below;

Naija News recalls that Vee who happens to be the fourth runner-up in the show was Laycon’s closest friend in the house and was there to discuss urgent issues with him when he was turned down by Erica.
