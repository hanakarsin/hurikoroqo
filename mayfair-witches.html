'Mayfair Witches' Latest News and Updates

The AMC original series Mayfair Witches, based on Anne Rice's trilogy — The Lives of the Mayfair Witches — is part of the Network's Immortal Universe. The Immortal Universe is a television franchise that will span centuries covering the late gothic writer's greatest literary accomplishments.

The series follows Dr. Rowan Fielding (Alexandra Daddario): a young neurosurgeon who discovers she is an heir to a powerful line of witches. She must come to terms with her powers while facing a threatening presence that has plagued her family for generations.

Network: AMC
