Again, House Of Rep. Loses Another Member

A member of the House of Representatives, Suleiman Aliu, has been reported dead.

Reports reaching Naija News at this time confirmed that the lawmaker representing Lere Federal Constituency, Kaduna State under the All Progressives Congress (APC), gave up the ghost on Monday, April 5.

Aliu was said to have passed on at the Barau Dikko Teaching Hospital in Kaduna following a brief illness.

Though the nature of the sickness that claimed the lawmaker’s life is yet to be confirmed, Naija News understands that Aliu’s demise is coming in the wake of a fatal accident that claimed the life of Haruna Maitala.
