Sjon - Age, Family, Bio

Icelandic poet, author, and lyricist who is known for From the Mouth of the Whale, The Blue Fox, I've Seen It All, and other works.

Before Fame

He published his first poetry collection, Synir, in 1978, when he was sixteen years old.

Trivia

He co-founded Medusa, a cultural group in the neo-Surrealist tradition.

Family Life

He settled in Reykjavik with his wife and two children.

Associated With

He and singer Bjork collaborated on their first project -- a song called "Isobel" -- in 1995.
