UPS Kenya: parcel tracking, prices, contacts

UPS Kenya is a company that deals with the shipment of parcels within the country and beyond. The company provides more services that are aimed at ensuring that the customer’s interests are fully meant. Among these services is the UPS tracking service. UPS tracking helps build the customer’s confidence while shipping their parcels from the origin to the intended destination.

How does UPS Kenya work?

Importing goods from overseas is no longer a big deal, thanks to companies such as UPS Kenya. The company will ensure that your shipment, whether big or small, gets to you safely. UPS Kenya has a tracking service that allows you to know the location of your shipment as time progresses until when your parcel gets to your address. UPS Kenya parcel tracking

UPS tracking is flexible. You can choose to track your parcel using a tracking number. Alternatively, you can also use a tracking reference number. If you opt to use the reference number, in most cases, it can be the customer number or the purchase number. The reference number should consist of thirty-five characters.

UPS Kenya also has another added advantage. It keeps your tracking history details. The system allows storing the history of up to 75 most recent shipments. It also keeps the tracking number for each one of these tracking history records.

How does UPS tracking work

You can track your UPS shipment using a reference number.

When creating a shipment, you can assign a reference, which is similar to a purchase order number, or customer number. This number allows you to track your shipments without a tracking number.

You must be wondering what is the difference between a tracking number and a tracking/shipment reference number? Well, here is the difference:

  • Tracking/shipment reference number - is an identifier that you define by completing the reference fields during the shipping process. This reference number could either, be a purchase order number, a customer number, a company name, a Bill of Lading number, or a phrase that identifies that shipment - such as "10 crates" or "gift for Mom." Shipment references can be any combination of letters and numbers up to 35 characters.
  • Tracking number - UPS tracking number is a number automatically assigned to every package. Both the customer and the company can use this tracking number to locate the package in the system and determine its delivery status and other relevant details.

UPS Kenya price

UPS Kenya prices are not constant. They are dependent on the weight of the parcel. For this case, the weight refers to the billable weight. The billable weight is obtained by diving the volume of the parcel, in cubic centimeters, by five thousand. The value obtained is the rounded off to the nearest kilogram. Their prices are also dependent on the zone where the parcel is being sent to. The price is exclusive of any other charges and taxes. It is therefore vital to ensure that you factor in all these charges before finally paying the cost to avoid any forms of inconveniences.
