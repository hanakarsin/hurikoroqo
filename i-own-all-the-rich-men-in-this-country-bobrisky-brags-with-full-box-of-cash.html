'I Own All The Rich Men In This Country'

Controversial Nigerian crossdresser, Idris Okuneye, popularly called Bobrisky has taken to social media to show off a box full of money whilst claiming ownership of all the rich men in the country.

Naija News reports that the male barbie on Wednesday via his Snapchat showed off boxes of money while advising young women to open their hearts to love and learn to be themselves so as to enable them enjoy the fancy things of life.

“I got a lot of messages from many girls saying they are lonely that they need love.. Sweethearts just open your heart and be yourself, honestly, someone will come soon. Don’t push it.”

He also noted that his flamboyant lifestyle display on social media is nothing but a daily rountine and not to oppress his massive followers.

“Public Announcement.. Please don’t see my post here as an oppression or whatever, I’m just a lovely sweet soul posting my daily lifestyle and nothing more. If you feel hurt, I’m sorry.”

Bobrisky then went further to reveal why he’s always showing off huge wads of cash on social media.  According to him, he tries to avoid any trace of transfer due to the prying media eyes and gossip.

“There is this general question people ask me, bob why are you always with cash don’t your boyfriend do transfer.. My boyfriend is a boyfriend is a billionaire and a famous one, as a matter of fact, he more famous, and we are trying to avoid any trace of transfer to my account cos of gossip. So most times he does cash in dollars or naira cos we feel that way is better.”

Sharing the box of cash, the controversial crossdresser wrote; “No girl can compete with me, trust me.. i own all the rich men in this country lowkey”
