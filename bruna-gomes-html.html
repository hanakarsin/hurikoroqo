Bruna Gomes - Age, Family, Bio

Brazilian Instagram star whose photos of her beauty, fashion, and lifestyle have amassed her 6 million followers. She creates promotional content for her upcoming projects and uploads them to the popular bru page. She frequently posts Instagram reels, including one of her most popular which featured a hack to brushing hair with floss. 

Before Fame

Her first Instagram post was on March 20, 2016, and was a selfie video of her lip synching. The caption read "Opa". 

Trivia

She launched a YouTube channel in May 2018, with a vlog titled "OI PISSUAL". She has 2.9 million subscribers. She has appeared on many reality TV shows including the Big Brother franchise. 

Family Life

She began dating Felipe Neto in 2017 but they announced their breakup in December 2021. She has been romantically linked to Bernardo Sousa.

Associated With

She is friends with people like Raissa Chaddad and Lorrayne Mavromatis.
