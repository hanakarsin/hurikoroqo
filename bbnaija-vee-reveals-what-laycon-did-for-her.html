Vee Reveals What Laycon Did For Her

A housemate in the Big Brother Naija House, ‘Lockdown Edition’, Vee, on Friday disclosed the things the winner of the reality show, Laycon did for her in the house.

The fourth runner-up in the show was Laycon’s closest friend in the house and was there to discuss urgent issues with him when he was turned down by Erica.

Vee in her statement noted that Laycon changed her mind about a lot of thigs, she added that her time with the reality show winner was a blessing.

She revealed that Laycon saved her from a lot of arguments while in the house.

Vee said, “Laycon has got an inviting spirit, I can see a lot of people talking about things our friendship has done to him, what they ain’t saying is what our friendship has done to me.”

“He has changed my mind about a lot of things, meeting him in the house is a blessing.”

“I could have been worse, a lot more quarrels, but he was there for me too.”
