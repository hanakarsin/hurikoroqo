Davido And Chioma Reportedly Welcome Twins In The US

Nigerian superstar singer, David Adeleke, better known as Davido has reportedly welcomed a set of twins with his celebrity chef wife, Chioma.

Naija News gathered that Davido and Chioma welcomed their new babies on Monday in the United States of America.

According to sources quoted by PM News, the twins arrived at about 9pm on Monday.

Though neither Davido nor his wife have so far confirmed the news, a popular Nigerian clergy, Evangelist Gospel Agochukwu shared a hint about the delivery in a post on his Church’s official Facebook page on Tuesday morning.

The cleric uploaded his supposed chat with Davido.

Davido Reacts As AY Apologizes After Manhood Joke

In other news, Davido, has accepted comedian AY Makun‘s apology over a joke he made about the former’s private part.

The 52-year-old tendered his apology on Sunday with a picture of the musician and videos of the duo posted on Instagram, noting that he deserves the backlash he was getting for it.

Recall, AY during the Warri Again Festival held on October 6 said that Davido was impregnating women with his “small pr**k” while it took him (AY) 13 years to impregnate his wife.

Speaking about the Joke, AY took to his social media page to reveal that he was sorry.

Davido replied to the post, implying in Pidgin that he had forgiven the comedian.
