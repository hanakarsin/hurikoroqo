Teebillz Crowns Tacha Queen, Says She Is The Only Bbnaija Housemate Who Has Not Opened Her Legs

Media Mogul and manager Tunji Balogun also known as Teebillz has praised Tacha as the only successful Bbnaija housemate that has not opened her legs.

Naija News reports that Teebillz took to his Instagram account to make the declaration.

In the post on Friday, he also thanked her for allowing him make the difference in her life.

He wrote: “I’ve patiently waited after 2 seasons of BBN to see the most bankable without opening them legs! I’m thankful to you Anita for allowing me to make the difference for what I do best……. Proud of you my love!”

Teebillz began to manage Tacha after she was disqualified from the big brother house in September 2019.

During a chat on “Talks with Ubi” Tunji stated that when he got in touch with Tacha for the first time it was initially to check up on her and make sure she was okay due to the consistent backlash she was receiving online but after speaking to her he realized she had potential.

Meanwhile, Nollywood actress Adunni Ade has taken to her social media to recount how her father reacted when she got pregnant outside wedlock years ago.

The 39-year-old in a lengthy post on Instagram disclosed that she went through a lot when she found out that she was pregnant with her first child.
