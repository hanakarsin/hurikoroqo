Kenyans express mixed reactions after Justina Syokau is spotted with heavily built bodyguards

- Syokau hired some bodyguards for an event she had been invited to recently

- Photos of her with her bodyguards sparked mixed reactions online with many people condemning her for not trusting in God's protection

- Some people defended her holding she is free to do whatever she wants with her life

The Kenyan music audience is a very complicated one, and this can be attested by many players in the industry.

Music fans can make one an instant star but they can also make one lose it all overnight.

In essence, these are people who will support you and pray for your success but still turn against you, should you succeed past their expectations.

The aforementioned is exactly what just happened to gospel singer Justina Syokau whose fans recently picked issue with her for doing a normal thing in ''celebriville''.

In 2020, the singer, famed for Twendi Twendi blew up thanks to the support of many Kenyans who pushed her projects on different social media platforms.

Many people who supported the growth of the lass expected her to maintain the humble lifestyle she has always shown ever since her breakthrough.

Well, the lass became a big star, a status that forced her to embrace a different lifestyle, including her wardrobe and other important things to fit her celebrity status.

Justina recently attended a local event and thought it wise to hire security guards to protect her during the whole thing.

She then shared photos of the same on her social media in a move that attracted massive criticism from a portion of her fans.

Many people who camped on the comments section of her post condemned her for trying to pull celebrity moves and forgetting about her humble background.
