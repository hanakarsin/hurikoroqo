Cubana Chief Priest Gifts Boy In Viral 'Dirty Look Video Half A Million, Makes More Promise

Many days after receiving backlash on social media, Pascal Chibuike, popularly known as Cubana Chief Priest, has defended his action against a boy in a viral ‘dirty look’ video.

Naija News learnt that the show promoter and businessman gifted the young man the sum of five hundred thousand [N500,000] to encourage his love for entertainment.

Recall that a video had gone viral weeks ago showing Cubana Chief Priest giving a ‘dirty look’ to a boy who he met at an event.

Naija News understands that the boy had initiated a selfie video trying to capture a moment with the celebrity barman.

Having noticed what the boy was doing, Cubana Chief Priest gave the boy a demeaning look and walked away from the recording.

Nigerians, however, took to social media to condemn the socialite, describing his actions as unnecessary.

It is understood that the young man and Cubana Chief Priest were both on a stage while preparing for a show when the incident was recorded.

Defending his actions, Cubana said the boy who was employed to set up his venue, abandoned the job he was paid for and was playing around.

However, weeks after a series of backlash following the event, Cubana Chief Priest, during an Instagram live video, jokingly scolded the boy and promised to bless him with N500,000.

The entertainer was said to have also promised to hook up with the boy in Lagos to spoil him a little.
