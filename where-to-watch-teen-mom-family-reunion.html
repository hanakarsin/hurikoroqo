Here's Where to Watch MTV's Teen Mom: Family Reunion

Don't Miss Out! Here's How to Watch 'Teen Mom: Family Reunion'

​​New episodes of the wildly popular reality series 'Teen Mom: Family Reunion' air Wednesdays at 8 p.m. EST on MTV.

By Leila Kozma

Updated April 25 2024, 3:34 p.m. ET

If there’s one thing MTV will do, it’s bring back its popular reality TV personalities! In 2022, the network unveiled Teen Mom: Family Reunion, a spinoff featuring stars from three of its hit reality shows: Teen Mom, Teen Mom 2, and Teen Mom: Young and Pregnant.

With familiar faces like Maci McKinney, Jade Cline, Catelynn Baltierra, and many others, Teen Mom: Family Reunion shines a spotlight on both friendships and feuds.

If you're itching to dive into the juicy drama, here's how you can watch Teen Mom: Family Reunion — streaming options included!

Here's how to watch or stream 'Teen Mom: Family Reunion.'

The easiest way to keep up with the latest (and dare we say, rowdiest) occurrences taking place on Teen Mom: Family Reunion is by tuning in on Wednesdays at 8 p.m. EST. Each new episode of the show airs on MTV, promising an exceptional opportunity for drama-hungry fans to grab some popcorn and soak it all in.

You can also stream live if you choose a live TV plan on Philo, Fubo, Sling, Hulu, Vidgo, or DirecTV. Additionally, you can use most of these providers to DVR new episodes of Teen Mom: Family Reunion.

For those who prefer to leave decisions until the very last minute, the live TV plans on Philo, Fubo, Sling, Vidgo, and DirecTV all offer different kinds of free trials of various lengths.

Philo, Fubo, and Vidgo offer seven days; Sling's "Orange," "Blue," or "Orange + Blue" bundles (where you can stream MTV) offer three days; and DirecTV offers five days.

Or, if you prefer the binge-watching experience, hold off until the entire season wraps up on MTV, as it will soon be available on Paramount Plus. Then, you can enjoy the entire season at your own pace without having to wait for a new episode every week!

​​New episodes of Teen Mom: Family Reunion air Wednesdays at 8 p.m. EST on MTV.
