Brimstone - Age, Family, Bio

Will Kaye, or as he is better known, Brimstone, is a professional wrestler and radio host recognized as the voice of iHeartRadio's The Grindhouse Radio. He is also the CEO of Hound Comics and has been a spokesperson for the Stan Lee Foundation. 

Before Fame

Before he began his pro wrestling career in 1996, he'd been a drummer for bands like Image, Who's Laughing Now, and Bugsy. As a child, he was also an actor, appearing in episodes of Sesame Street and Romper Room. 

Trivia

He has been the President of WK Promotions Corp. for more than two decades and he also founded both the Critical Mass Pro Wrestling, Corp. and New York Wrestling Connection. 

Family Life

His real name is William Kucmierowski. He married Danielle Kucmierowski in 2009, and they have three children. 

Associated With

He has been trained by a number of wrestlers including Low Ki.  He got the name for his signature wrestling move, "XiBalba" from Dee Snider s 1998 film Strangeland.
