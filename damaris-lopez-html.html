Damaris Lopez - Age, Family, Bio

Dominican-American model and video vixen from Drake's "Hotline Bling," 2 Chainz' "I'm different," Rick Ross' "Gallardo" and many other music videos. She has been a part of numerous editorial, runway and promotional campaigns as well.

Before Fame

She first started working when she was 15 years old, and was providing for her entire home by the time she was 18.

Trivia

She has a huge following on Instagram of more than 170,000.

Family Life

Her father passed away in 2001. She and her sister Eliza were then raised by Damaris Maldonado.

Associated With

She was the lead girl in the music video for Yo Gotti's "Check."
