Apple TV Plus Latest News and Updates

The on-demand streaming platform service Apple TV Plus launched in 2019 and can be accessed through Apple's website and the Apple TV app.

Apple TV Plus is available in 100 countries and as of July 2021 has a reported 20 million subscribers.

In 2021, the Apple TV Plus series Ted Lasso won four Golden Globe nominations. Apple TV Plus also has production deals with major celebrities including Oprah Winfrey, Prince Harry, Leonardo DiCaprio, and Jon Stewart.
