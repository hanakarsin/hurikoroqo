Mike De Force - Age, Family, Bio

Husband of internet personality Fleur De Force who stars alongside his wife on the web series Food With Fleur & Mike on the Sainsbury's Food channel. He has over 30,000 followers on Twitter. 

Before Fame

He and Fleur met at a house party of a friend. He works for his family's car dealership.

Trivia

He became engaged to Fleur on June 26, 2012 and they married August 24, 2013.

Family Life

He has a twin sister named Clare and another sister named Lizzie. He and Fleur welcomed their first child together in December of 2017. They welcomed a son named Enzo on July 18th, 2022.

Associated With

He has been featured on Fleur's YouTube channel like Luke Conard, MissGlamorazzi and others.
