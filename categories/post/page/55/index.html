Nigeria Has Nothing To Celebrate Under Buhari LP

published on July 14, 2024
The Labour Party (LP) has said the country does not have anything to celebrate in the last seven years of the Muhammadu Buhari government. NURTW Boss, MC Oluomo Bags Honorary Doctorate Degree

published on July 14, 2024
The Lagos state chairman of the National Union of Road Transport Workers NURTW Musiliu Ayinde Akinsanya better known as MC Oluomo has bagged an Honorary Doctorate Degree. The Nigerian socialite was awarded a doctorate in Corporate Professional from the Institute of Corporate and Public Sector Professionals. According to the photo of the certificate posted by Oluomo on his social media page, the honorary degree was dated November 2021. Why I Changed My Style Of Music From Rap To Afrobeats Victony

published on July 14, 2024
Sensational Nigerian singer, Anthony Ebuka Victor, popularly known as Victony, has disclosed that he changed his style of music from hip-hop to afrobeats because he wanted to “try something new.” The songwriter explained that the pandemic quarantine gave him the opportunity to discover himself. Victony stated this during an interview with Nelson CJ covered by Teen Vogue’s Africa’s New Wave. “The aura, the feeling during lockdown was very different, and that prompted me to want to try something new,” the ‘Soweto’ crooner said.
