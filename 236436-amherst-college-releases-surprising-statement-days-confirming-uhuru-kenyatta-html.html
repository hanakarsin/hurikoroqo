Amherst College releases surprising statement days after confirming Uhuru Kenyatta graduated

- No more responding to individual inquiries about Uhuru's degree

- The statement comes after Kenyans took to Twitter in overwhelming numbers to demand to know about the president degree’s claiming that he has ruined Kenyan's economy

Amherst College, a US college that President Uhuru Kenyatta graduated from, has released a statement saying that it won’t respond to individual inquiries any more about Uhuru Kenyatta’s university education and graduation.

The statement comes after Kenyans took to Twitter in overwhelming numbers to demand to know about the president's economics degree claiming that he has ruined the country's economy.

“How President Uhuru Kenyatta got his economics degree @AmherstCollege is wanting,” tweeted a Kenyan identified as Sururu Salaash. “He is getting it wrong on everything economic!”

“The guy has killed our economy, that’s why we want to know if indeed he studied there,” tweeted Mongare Richard.
