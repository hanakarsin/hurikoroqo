Man secretly divorces wife using her fake signature

A Texas based man is currently on police radar after divorcing his wife without her knowledge.

Paul Nixon reportedly filed for a divorce alone and went ahead to conclude the process without informing his wife.

Nixon, 51, allegedly divorced his wife by forging documents behind her back.

TUKO.co.ke understands the divorce process involves both parties mutually agreeing to part ways.

However, Nixon came up with fake papers and forwarded them to the authorities all in a bid to call off his marriage with his embattled wife.

According to the divorced wife who spoke to the media, the court had already completed all the hearing proceedings and filed the final divorce decree using fake documents which had her counterfeited signature.

New York police noted that Nixon violated the law when he decided to go through with the divorce process without his wife's knowledge.

It is important to note for a divorce procedure to be deemed successfully, both parties are supposed to reach an agreement, something Nixon did not consider.

Police went ahead to reveal the wife found out she had been divorced without her consent after she confronted the husband over jewelry purchase.

It is then that Nixon informed her that they were officially divorced.

The two had been married for only a couple of years before he chose to divorce her without notice.

The divorce decree was, however, cancelled as the husband had fraudulently ran the process.

Police also launched a manhunt for Nixon who is wanted for perjury and has a no-bond warrant out for his arrest.
