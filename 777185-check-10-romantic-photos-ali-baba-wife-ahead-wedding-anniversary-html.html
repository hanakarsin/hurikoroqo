See these 10 romantic photos of Ali Baba and wife (photos)

Veteran Nigerian comedian Ali Baba and wife Mary Akporobomerere has got everyone sending out wedding anniversary wishes for him on the social media platform.

The comedian ahead of his 10-years wedding anniversary on Thursday, March 24, posted a picture with the inscription: “REAL MEN MARRY EVENT MANAGERS.” Which so many has translated that, Ali Baba is using the medium to celebrate his wife. He posted that with the caption:

“Any woman you marry is your event manager! She can make your life eventful or boring. And as event Managers go, if your life is an A class event, if you use a C category event planner… you can’t be expecting an A class life ooo. That said, the operational words here are #realmen, marry an event managers… Apply wisdom.”

Going through Ali Baba’s Instagram page, so many pictures of him and his beautiful wife has been rolling in as they want to celebrate the comedy star ahead of his wedding anniversary coming up in April 15.
