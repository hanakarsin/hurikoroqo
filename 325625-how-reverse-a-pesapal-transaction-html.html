How to reverse a PesaPal transaction

Occasionally, you will need to return funds to a customer after a transfer. With the Sabi app, the process is straightforward. Here is the fastest and convenient way of how to reverse money sent to the Sabi mPOS Account. The funds will be refunded to the original account from which it was transferred.

  • Get to your Sabi App
  • On the platform, locate the navigation panel
  • Click on "View Activity". All your financial transfers will appear
  • Identify the transfer you want to reverse and select it. The transaction page will appear next.
  • Select the "Issue Refund", a green button. You will be required to permit the reverse
  • Click "Yes"
