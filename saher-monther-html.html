Saher Monther - Age, Family, Bio

Popular internet personality and content creator who is best known for his Saher Monther YouTube channel where he posts commentary, lifestyle and reaction videos and vlogs. His content has earned him over 360,000 subscribers and 56 million views on the platform.    

Before Fame

He was bullied as a child for wearing makeup. Being bullied was part of his motivation to start a YouTube channel. He launched his YouTube channel in July 2018. 

Trivia

He has gained over 750,000 followers on his sahermonther Instagram account, where he posts a range of stylized pictures of himself. He is recognized by fans for his spontaneous personality and for wearing makeup. 

Family Life

He is of Lebanese and Syrian descent. He was born in Golan Heights, Syria. 

Associated With

He and ASMR Glow are both popular YouTube stars from Israel.
