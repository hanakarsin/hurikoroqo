Gab Smolders - Age, Family, Bio

Dutch gaming personality who is widely known for her Gab Smolders YouTube channel, which was formerly called GirlGamerGaB. She has gained popularity there for her Let's Plays and playthroughs for a number of horror games, including Silent Hill and The Witch's House.

Before Fame

She began her YouTube channel on September 23, 2014.

Trivia

She has amassed more than 750,000 subscribers to her YouTube channel. She has released her own line of clothing through the TeePublic brand.

Family Life

She was born and raised in the Netherlands. She has two older sisters and an older brother. She began dating Jacksepticeye in 2019.

Associated With

She has been a frequent gaming collaborator of John Wolfe on his HarshlyCritical YouTube channel.
