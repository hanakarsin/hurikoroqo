Eniola Badmus Warns Those Making Promises To Obama DMW's Son Not To Fail

Nollywood actress Eniola Badmus has taken to social media to warn those persons, particularly celebrities making promises to Obama DMW’s son, not to fail. 

Naija News Naija News earlier reported that Obama DMW, a close associate of pop singer Davido, had died of a suspected heart attack.

Late Obama DMW, born Habeeb Uthman, reportedly drove himself to a hospital in Lekki, Lagos State on Tuesday to complain that he was feeling unwell, but died later that day and buried the next day according to Islamic rites.

Some Nigerian celebrities, in a bid to console late Obama DMW’s son, Abdul Uthman, made huge promises, ensuring to take care of him regardless his father’s death. 

However, the 43-year-old actress via her Instagram beseeched persons who have made various promises to Obama’s son to ensure they do not fail. 

Taking to her instastories, she wrote; I see so many people writing RIP cigarette or i got you Malik (Obama’s son) for life!! #Letsnotpromiseandfail
