'Your Eye No Clear' - Annie Idibia Reacts To Pregnancy Speculations

Nollywood actress, Annie Idibia, has reacted to speculations that she is expecting a third child following a recent post shared on her social media page.

Naija News reports that the actress took to her Instagram page to share a video of herself, rocking a black dress.

However, some people who spotted her protruding belly assumed she might be pregnant and took to the comment section to question her.

dukeofbarking wrote: “Nne you get belly again abi my eyes no clear?”

artofgoldbyriri wrote: “Am I the only one seeing a bump”

misscooperdee wrote: “Your eye is very clear congratulations to her”

toria_emma wrote: “I think say na only me see am ooooooo”

worldclassfigure1 wrote: “I been think say this weather naim dey affect my eyes, I saw it o”

However, Annie responded to one of the inquisitive followers, insinuating that she is not pregnant.

She wrote: “Your eyes no clear ooo”

Sabinus Replaces Damaged Black Benz

Meanwhile, Nigerian skit maker, Emmanuel Chukwuemeka Ejekwu, popularly known as Oga Sabinus, has purchased another Mercedez Benz weeks after his new Benz was damaged.

It would be recalled that the internet sensation was involved in an accident after holding a secret engagement in the Ojo area of Lagos state.

Although, the comedian survived the incident unscathed, his Benz was badly damaged.

However, weeks after the incident, the Instagram comedian in the video shared on social media, was seen acquiring another Mercedes Benz GLE. 

Sabinu, who was unable to control his excitement, thanked the dealer and drove off with his new luxury car.
