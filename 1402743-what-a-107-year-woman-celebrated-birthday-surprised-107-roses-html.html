What a 107-year-old woman who celebrated her birthday looks like and how she was surprised with 107

- A centenarian has recently added a new age to her years as she clocked 107 years

- The woman identified as Elestine Lawson got a rare birthday gift that got her surprised

- She was gifted 107 roses, the exact number of roses that matched her age, by a local flower shop

People all over the world celebrate, as special, the day of their birth, with some throwing lavish parties, while others would rather use that as an opportunity to give to the less privilege.

And there are those that don't do anything beyond celebrating themselves with posts on social media.

An old woman has been treated to a birthday gesture that made her celebration of a new age memorable.

The Ohio-based lady identified as Elestine Lawson, according to Blacknews, recently celebrated her 107th birthday and was surprised by a local flower shop with 107 roses.

Black Culture News reports that the centenarian, and her family, received at their door 107 roses from Battiste LaFleur Galleria, a florist shop in Columbia.

She would, reportedly, learn later on that the surprise was planned by some members of her church.

As to the secret to her longevity of life, Elestine said that her love and faith have always kept her going.
