Kristen Espinosa - Age, Family, Bio

Social media personality and sister of social media superstar Matt Espinosa, she also works as a nutritionist, health and wellness coach, and business entrepreneur. She has over 30,000 followers on her popular Instagram account. 

Before Fame

She grew up loving to sing, dance, act, and perform on stage. 

Trivia

She and her little brothers, including Matt, used to make lip sync and dance videos together for fun. She earned a master's degree in nutrition in spring 2015. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Woodbridge, Virginia. She has two other brothers apart from Matt, named Dylan and Ryan. 

Associated With

Her brother Matt became a member of the group Magcon Boys along with Aaron Carpenter.
