Khamani Griffin - Age, Family, Bio

American actor best known for his roles in the TV series All of Us and in the movie Daddy Day Care. Both performances earned him nominations for Young Artist Awards. He appears on the TV series titled, "The Mick" in 2017.

Before Fame

He began performing at the age of two and started reading at the age of three. He played football at the University of Southern California.

Trivia

His voice acting credits include roles in Happy Feet and The Little Engine That Could.

Family Life

He was born in Oakland, California, and grew up with a younger sister, Aniela, and an older brother, De'Von Flournoy.

Associated With

He appeared alongside Eddie Murphy in the 2003 comedic film Daddy Day Care.
