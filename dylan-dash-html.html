Dylan Chloe Dash - Trivia, Family, Bio

Actress most largely recognized for her role as Tara in the 2023 Netflix original comedy film You Are So Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah. The film was released to significant critical and fan acclaim. It starred Adam Sandler, his wife Jackie Sandler, and their two daughters Sadie Sandler and Sunny Sandler.

Before Fame

She made her professional acting debut portraying the titular character in the short comedy film Adeline, the Great in the year 2019.

Trivia

She has shared photos of her travels to international hotspots such as Italy, Spain, and France on her dylan.dash Instagram account. She has earned more than 30,000 followers on the growing page.

Family Life

She is from the United States.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Idina Menzel and Samantha Lorraine in the 2023 comedy film You Are So Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah.
