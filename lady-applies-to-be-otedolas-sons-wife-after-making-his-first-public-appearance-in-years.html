Lady Applies To Be Otedolas Sons Wife After Making His First Public Appearance In Years

A Nigerian lady has proposed marriage to the son of billionaire businessman Femi Otedola, Fewa, after he made his first public appearance during the 60th birthday celebration of his dad in a private yacht.

The lady simply known as Toun_c  on TikTok, appealed to Nigerians via the video app to help her send her request across to the Otedola family.

According to Toun, she doesn’t mind if Fewa is a person with special needs, as she has her own needs and wouldn’t mind collaborating so that can both benefit from the relationship.

She added that she is ready to marry the billionaire’s son.

“Can someone help me check, kindly if Otedola’s son is old enough to get married.

“I don’t care if that boy is a boy with special need, I am girl with special need as well. He will scratch my back and I will scratch his.

“Please help me call Femi that I’m ready to marry his son,” she wrote

Fewa, Naija News understands is the only son of Femi Otedola, and was present during his father’s birthday, sharing food and drinks with everyone on board the yacht, excitedly.

Unlike his sisters, Olawumi, DJ Cuppy, and Temi Otedola, Fewa likes to shy away from public functions.

Fewa who is autistic (has challenges with socializing with people) is the only male heir to his father’s billion-dollar fortune.
