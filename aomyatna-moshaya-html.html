Eman Moshaya - Age, Family, Bio

One member of the YouTube vlogging family mmoshaya with her parents and siblings. The insanely popular family channel has more than 23 million subscribers for their vlogs, challenges, and sketch videos.

Before Fame

The mmoshaya Youtube channel was created in December 2010 and the first video was posted a month later.

Trivia

The Moshaya Family Instagram account has a monumental following of more than 1.9 million fans. One of their most popular compilation videos has garnered more than 240 million views. 

Family Life

Her father runs the mmoshaya channel. She is joined on the channel by her three siblings, Anas, Yusuf, and Jad. 

Associated With

She and her family visited Disneyland in Paris, France in October of 2021.
