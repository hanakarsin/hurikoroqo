Zahra Buhari Receives Award - Legit.ng

The beautiful daughter of President Muhammadu Buhari Zahra has on December 10, Thursday received an award from National Oracle Magazine as the Charity Ambassador of the year 2015.

Zahra took to twitter to share photos of herself with the award.

[article_adwert]

Nigerians reacted to the news and commended the president’s daughter:

Buhari’s daughter has been signed as an ambassador-at-large for a charity organization in Nigeria called ACE in August.

ACE (Assisting caring and empowering) charity is a non-profit organisation whose mission is to re-define the meaning of charity in Africa while providing education, literacy skills and training to African children and youths.

Zahra is famous for her active social position. Recently she spoke on the death penalty for corrupt officials.
