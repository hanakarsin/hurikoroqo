Nyeri Governor Wahome Gakuru is dead

- Another Nyeri governor died while still in office

- Governor Wahome Gakuru passed away on Tuesday, November 7, following a freak accident

- The first Nyeri governor, the late Nderitu Gachagua, also passed on while still in office

Nyeri Governor Wahome Gakuru is dead.

The late Gakuru was the third legislator to hold the office of Governor in Nyeri county.

Gakuru passed on following a tragic road accident on Tuesday, November 7, in Kabati along the Thika-Kenol Highway in Murang'a county.

According to police reports, the vehicle got a tyre burst while being driven in heavy rains.

According to prior TUKO.co.ke reports, Wahome was in the vehicle with two other occupants when the early morning accident occurred.

The governor was heading to Nairobi to attend a talk show accompanied by his personal assistant, his bodyguard and driver when the vehicle he was travelling in veered off the road and hit a rail guard

TUKO.co.ke understands that the guard rail penetrated his stomach and the politician stood little chance of survival

The late Gakuru won the Nyeri gubernatorial seat in the August 8 General Election, beating former governor Samuel Wamathai and Munene Kairu.

During a past rally in Nyeri, Gakuru was quoted saying that it was by the grace of God that he won the gubernatorial seat after his opponents ran fierce smear campaigns against him.

The late Gakuru beat Wamathai after the latter assumed office upon the death of the first Nyeri governor; Nderitu Gachagua.
