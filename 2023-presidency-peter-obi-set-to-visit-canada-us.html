Peter Obi Set To Visit Canada, US

The presidential candidate of Labour Party (LP), Peter Obi, is set to visit the United States of America (USA) and Canada ahead of the 2023 general elections.

Naija News understands that the former Governor of Anambra State will be in Los Angeles, California; Houston, Texas and New York to engage Nigerians in Diaspora on civil leadership.

The LP presidential candidate will also be in Charlotte, North Carolina; Washington DC, and Atlanta to speak with Nigerians in Diaspora on civil leadership.

Obi will be in Toronto in Canada to meet with some Nigerians in Diaspora in a Town Hall meeting.

According to a poster shared by the Peter Obi Support Network, Obi will begin his tour on Sunday, August 28 and end on Sunday, September 4, 2022.

Atiku To Meet Wike In London As Associates Fume Over Lamido, Babangida’s Attack On Rivers Governor

The presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar, is set to hold a meeting with Governor Nyesom Wike of Rivers State.

Naija News had earlier reported that the former Vice-President on Monday jetted out to London, UK, to meet Wike.

Atiku travelled out of the country on Monday after attending the Annual General Conference of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) in Lagos.

Sources have now revealed that Atiku would meet Wike in an attempt to resolve his rift with Nyesom Wike.

Sources close to the former vice-president said Abubakar is billed to meet with the Rivers governor on Thursday (today).
