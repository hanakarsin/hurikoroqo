Highly Anticipated 'X' Sequel 'MaXXXine' May Be Just Around the Corner

A24's 'X' sequel, 'MaXXXine,' has reportedly been sent for limited test screenings. When is the film's release date?

Fans of A24 horror movies were in for a bloody surprise when slasher X and its prequel, Pearl, both released within the same year. Director Ti West, along with starring actor Mia Goth, created and shot both films at the same time, transforming 1970s Texas into a World War I fever dream.

After's Pearl's credits, viewers were treated to a teaser for the third movie in the franchise — MaXXXine. So, given that the first two movies released in such quick succession, when can we expect to see the highly anticipated sequel to X hit theaters? Was it filmed at the same time as its predecessors? Here's what we know.

When is 'MaXXXine's' release date? It could be sooner than we think.

With X hitting theaters in March 2022 and Pearl following not long after in September 2022, viewers were hoping that the trilogy's finale, MaXXXine, would be released in early 2023 — but to the dismay of Maxine Minx fans everywhere, that wasn't the case.

However, recent reports have stated that the film has undergone limited test screenings in California, meaning that we could finally see it hit theaters soon.

According to a report from World of Reel, the film was shown to audiences in Burbank, Calif. on Dec. 8, 2023, with general reviews seeming positive. While the film team may still need a few months to incorporate any feedback they receive from the test screening or make final changes, this news means that MaXXXine will definitely be gracing our screens this year, and hopefully by summer.

It is worth noting that no official release date has been announced by A24, so any information is speculation at this point.

Here's what we know about 'MaXXXine's' filming locations.

While both X and Pearl took place on the same farm in rural Texas, neither was actually filmed in the U.S. at all. In fact, according to Screen Rant, Ti West did most of the filming for these movies in New Zealand. So, was MaXXXine filmed in the same location?

Taking place a few years after the events of X, MaXXXine is set in Hollywood, Calif., as Maxine Minx tries to find fame as a film star. According to reports by World of Reel, filming took place in Los Angeles, meaning that the movie's sets will likely be very true to the subject matter. As for specific filming locations, we will learn more as additional trailers are released.

The official 'MaXXXine' cast list has been announced.

To much excitement, A24 dropped another MaXXXine promo video on April 5, 2023 — and this time, it included the movie's cast list. Of course, Mia Goth will be reprising her titular role, but there will also be a plethora of familiar faces that fans didn't expect. Here's the lineup so far:

  • Mia Goth (Infinity Pool)
  • Lily Collins (Emily in Paris)
  • Elizabeth Debicki (The Crown)
  • Michelle Monaghan (Echoes)
  • Bobby Cannavale (The Watcher)
  • Giancarlo Esposito (Kaleidoscope)
  • Kevin Bacon (They/Them)
  • Halsey
  • Moses Sumney
