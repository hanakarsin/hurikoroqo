Sophie Elgort - Age, Family, Bio

Fashion photographer who is the sister of Ansel Elgort and the daughter of Arthur Elgort. She started a blog called Sundays with Soph and co-created a style column for The Financial Times called Suits and the City.

Before Fame

She was born in New York City and grew up around the sets of her photographer father where she developed photography as a hobby. She graduated from Brown University with a BA in Comparative Literature.

Trivia

Glamour, Paper, Surface, Teen Vogue, Tatler, Elle, Visual Tales, The Financial Times. Her advertising clients include Clinique, Etienne Aigner, Botkier, Tamara Mellon, ADEAM, Bloomingdales, Topshop, Theory, Rent the Runway, Buccellati and more.

Family Life

She has another sibling named Warren. She married private equity investor Eric Von Stroh.

Associated With

Another one of her advertising clients was Vince Camuto.
