Top 15 cute Nintendo characters that you will definitely love

Nintendo Company Limited is a Japanese multinational consumer electronics and video game company headquartered in Kyoto, Japan. The company is famous for its cute Nintendo characters that players can select from when they want to play. The organization was founded in 1889 and originally produced handmade playing cards.

Today, video games are more popular than ever, with an estimated 214 million people in the United States playing video games one hour or more per week. The success of most of these games lies in the level of interaction between the character and the player. So, what are the cutest Nintendo characters?

15 cute Nintendo characters that you will love

Nintendo's goal is to bring smiles to people's faces. One of the ways that the company does this is using its numerous video games and exciting characters. Each series has different personas, and netizens are regularly searching for Nintendo character pictures online. Here is a list of some of the cutest video game characters:

1. Mario

Also known as ''Mr Video Game,'' Mario is the most popular and coolest Nintendo character. Mario is always on a mission to rescue a missing princess or help a friend. Mario first appeared in Donkey Kong on the arcades as 'Jumpman' and later changed his name to Mario.

The SuperMario series enjoys a following of over 8 million fans on Facebook. Since his creation, Mario has appeared in over 200 games.

2. Petey Piranha

Petey is a sizeable mutant Piranha plant and was one of the first bosses on Super Mario Sunshine. He has also appeared as a villain boss character in Mario Pinball Land and Partners in Time. Petey also appears in several sports and spin-off games. Unlike other Piranhas, Petey can fly using his leaf-like arms and can create tornadoes.

3. Princess Daisy

Princess Daisy made her debut appearance in Super Mario Land as the ruler of Sarasaland. She is one of the cutest Mario characters. Daisy is likeable and down-to-earth and enjoys playing tennis and jumping over hurdles; hence many have described her as a tomboy.

The princess, who acts as a damsel in distress, was kidnapped by Tatanga before Mario rescued her. Daisy is portrayed as Mario's love interest and has appeared in several spinoffs.

4. Toad

Toad is the loyal servant of Princess Peach, who goes out of his way to protect her though he is often kidnapped as much as she is. He is also Mario's loyal and big-hearted friend.

Toad's debut appearance was in Super Mario Bros as one of the Mushroom Retainers, but his first playable character was in Super Mario Bros. 2. The character belongs to the Toad species, and they are all similar in appearance.

5. Toadette

Toadette also makes it to the list of cute cartoon characters on Nintendo. She is a female Toad who first appeared in the Nintendo GameCube game Mario Kart: Double Dash. In the game, she was an unlockable character. Toadette and Toad are close counterparts.

The tiny character is always ready to tackle any challenge. Toadette also loves adventure and fun and enjoys tracking treasure and crafting paper.

6. Luigi

Luigi is Mario's twin brother and is one of the protagonists of the Mario series. His first appearance was in Mario Bros. Unlike his brother, Luigi is a bit cowardly, wears green clothing and is taller than Mario.

Despite their differences in appearance, Luigi is always ready to help out his brother when the need arises. Luigi is also Mario's sidekick, and he first appeared in the 1983 Game & Watch game Mario Bros, where he is the character controlled by the second player.

7. Princess Peach

Princess Peach of the Mushroom Kingdom is also known as the princess in pink. She is one of the most popular characters in the series. Her first appearance was in Super Mario Bros, where Bowser kidnapped her because she was the only one capable of reversing his black magic, which he cast upon her territory.

8. Yoshi

Yoshi is a lovable anthropomorphic who first appeared in Super Mario World. Here, he assisted Mario and Luigi on their quest to save Dinosaur Island and the princess. Otherwise known as Yossy, he now has his series of games.

9. Princess Zelda

The brave Princess Zelda appears in the Legend of Zelda series. She is the ruler over Hyrule, and a descendant of the goddess Hylia, reincarnated as the first Zelda. She has appeared in different incarnations in every game to take a more active role. Zelda is the bearer of the Triforce of Wisdom and is good friends with Link.

10. Rosalina

Rosalina is a princess who first appeared in Super Mario Galaxy. She was a non-player who was the operator of the Observatory and the adoptive mother of the Lumas. Rosalina describes herself as the cosmos observer, protector of the Lumas and Mario's friend.

11. Shy guy

Shy Guy, known initially as Shyguys, are common enemies in the Mario and Yoshi series. They wear distinct masks because of their shyness. They first appeared in Yume Kojo: Doki Doki Panic and were later carried over to the Mario universe after Super Mario Bros 2 was released.

Shy Guys are unlockable in Mario Tennis and are common in spinoff games but are rare in the Super Mario series. Shy Guy is unlocked when the Star Cup is won in singles and is his first playable appearance. During the trophy ceremony, the character turns into a fly guy and starts flying around.

12. Boo

Boos are fictional ghosts that are also known as Teresa in Japan. They have unusually open mouths and are known for their large tongues and small arms. Their most distinctive feature is their shy personality, where they cover their faces when someone looks directly at them. However, they uncover their faces once the subject turns around.

King Boo is the leader of all the Boos and the main antagonist in both Luigi's Mansion and Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon. He also appears in other titles, such as Super Mario Sunshine.

13. Tetra

Tetra is a domineering pirate who leads a brilliant team searching for treasure in the high seas and carnal. At first encounter, many have dismissed her as greedy, but she has a golden heart and is frequently looking out for her friends.

The character appears in The Legend of Zelda series and Super Smash Bros series. Tetra, who is about 14 years old, is later revealed as Princess Zelda.

14. Koopa Troopa

Fans can also refer to them as Koopas or Troopas in the Mario series. They are turtle-like creatures with removable shells that come in different colours, especially red and green. Green Koopa Troopas walk back and forth without any concerns for pits or other obstacles, while Red Koopa Troopas walk back and forth without falling off their platforms.

15. Poochy

The cute Poochy appears on the Yoshi but is initially featured in Super Mario World 2: Yoshi's Island as Yoshi's friend. Poochy came through when Yoshi was unable to cross over particular spikes in the game. Yoshi would jump on top of Poochy's back and ride him over the points so that he could progress through the game.

Notably, Poochy is indestructible and can wade through the dangerous territory without a scratch. The character also appears in Tetris Attack and Mario Kart: Double Dash.
