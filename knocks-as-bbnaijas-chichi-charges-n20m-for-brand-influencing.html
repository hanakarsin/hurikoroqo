Knocks As BBNaija's Chichi Charges N20m For Brand Influencing

Former housemate of the Big Brother Naija season 7 ‘Level Up’ show Chinenyenwa Desire Okoebor, better known as Chichi has come under criticism after she announced what she charges for influencing.

This is coming after the reality star via her Instagram handle shared her charges and terms for partnership with brands interested in her influencing.

In the memo shared, the Edo-born revealed that for a one-year-brand influencing contract, she will be charging N20 million, while N12 million will be charged for 6 months.

However, her charges did not sit well with some netizens who called her out for charging too high.
