Crunchy Cat Luna - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok famous cat who has 4.5 million followers on her monkeycatluna account. She is known for the crunching noises she makes when she eats dry food. Her videos consist of her in a variety of situations, making funny poses, playing, or in general acting weird, always with a focus on her love for crunching. In August of 2022, she posted a video montage of her crunches which went on to reach over 34 million views.

Before Fame

She was born on May 30, 2019. Her TikTok debut came in November of 2020 in a video showing her unique catwalk. 

Trivia

Her monkeycatluna Instagram has 1.9 million followers. There are pictures of her making funny faces, leaping in the air, and celebrating her birthday, among others. There are also plenty of videos shared from her TikTok. There is a section of her Instagram dedicated to fan art made of Luna. 

Family Life

She was born in Greece. Her mother/owner runs her social media accounts. There is a TikTok video of her uncle trying to hold her in September of 2022, much to her dismay. 

Associated With

In an August of 2022 video, she posted a video about the influence of anime on her, particularly the show Death Note.
